# What is Recall?

**Recall** is a self-hosted AI platform purpose-built for enterprises that need to connect modern AI capabilities with legacy infrastructure. Whether your organization runs AS/400 systems, COBOL mainframes, or decades-old ERP platforms, Recall provides a secure, air-gapped bridge between those systems and state-of-the-art language models.

Built by [Recall AI](https://tryrecall.com) in Boca Raton, FL, Recall is designed for organizations where data sensitivity, compliance, and operational continuity are non-negotiable.

## Why Recall?

Most AI platforms assume a greenfield environment. Recall starts from the reality that enterprises run on systems built over decades — and those systems aren't going anywhere. Recall provides:

- **Secure connectivity** to legacy databases and APIs via sandboxed tool execution
- **Knowledge injection** so the AI understands your organization's terminology, processes, and constraints
- **Custom agents** tailored to specific workflows (data migration, report generation, system queries)
- **Full observability** with request tracing, tool call spans, and token usage tracking
- **OAuth integrations** with Google Workspace, GitHub, Microsoft 365, and Slack
